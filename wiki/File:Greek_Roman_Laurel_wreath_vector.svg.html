English: Vectorized laurel wreath in SVG format. Optimized for retina displays. Made in Adobe Illustrator CC 2014. Looks crisp on any screen

In Greek's Mithology: Symbol of victory awarded to victors in athletic competitions, including the ancient Olympics.

In the ancient Roman Empire: Symbol of martial triumph given to successful commanders.
