Carmanor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

In Greek mythology, Carmanor or Karmanor (Greek: Καρμάνωρ) may refer to the following personages:

  • Carmanor, a Cretan demi-god and consort of Demeter.[1]
  • Carmanor, son of Dionysus and Alexirrhoe.[2]
